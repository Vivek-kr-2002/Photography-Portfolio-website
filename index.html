Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket. He was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, India. Dhoni is considered one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and finishers in limited-overs cricket.

Dhoni made his international debut for India in December 2004 in an ODI against Bangladesh. He soon became known for his aggressive batting style and ability to finish matches. In 2007, he was appointed the captain of the Indian cricket team for the inaugural ICC World Twenty20, where India won the tournament, marking the beginning of his successful captaincy tenure.
